Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body turns food into energy. It occurs when your blood sugar levels are too high due to problems with insulin production or function.

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Hypertension is when the blood pressure in the arteries is too high. It can damage your heart, kidneys, and brain.

To prevent it: eat healthy, exercise regularly, reduce salt intake, and manage stress.
